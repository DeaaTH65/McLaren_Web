McLaren

McLaren Automotive is a British luxury automotive manufacturer based at the McLaren Technology Centre in Woking, England. The main products of the company are sports cars, which are produced in-house in designated production facilities. McLaren is now in the business of building high-performance road cars that are capable of putting Lamborghinis and Ferraris to shame. So far, McLaren is one of the few remaining automakers that hasn't produced an SUV, sticking exclusively to exotic sports cars and hypercars. The British automaker's products all carry prices in the six-figure range, including the plug-in-hybrid Artura, the gorgeous 720S, and the GT grand tourer.
